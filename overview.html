CONFERENCE OVERVIEW

The 1st international conference on "Novel sustainable practices in waste mitigation and its innovaive utilization" is organized by the department of civil engineering of Rajkiya Engineering College, Azamgarh. This conference aims at bringing together the industry and academia involved in the field of waste mitigation and its innovative utilization. The conference will emphasize on a two-way learning process i.e., from industry to academia and from academia to industry.

MODE OF CONFERENCE

Conference will be held online on virtual platform of Department of Civil Engineering (CED) located in Rajkiya Engineering College, Azamgarh, India.



WHO CAN ATTEND

Professionals in academia, construction industry, students and consulting organizations working on waste mitigation and its innovative utilization. The conference is open to all the common public and independent researchers.



PUBLICATIONS

The proceedings of the conference will be displayed on the official website of Rajkiya Engineering College, Azamgarh. The selected papers may be published in the forms of book with an ISBN number.
